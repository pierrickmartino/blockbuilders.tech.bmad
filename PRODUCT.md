# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Retail crypto traders who want to build, backtest, and iterate on trading
strategies without writing code. They understand markets but are not
developers. They use the app in focused sessions — building a strategy,
running a backtest, reviewing results — on desktop in a quiet workspace.

## Product Purpose

Blockbuilders is a no-code visual strategy lab. Users compose trading
strategies from blocks (indicators, logic, signals, risk rules), run
backtests against real OHLCV data, and monitor results over time.
Success means a trader can validate a strategy idea in minutes instead of
days, with full transparency into the backtest assumptions.

## Brand Personality

Distinctive, clear, structured. Not a toy, not a terminal. The interface
earns trust through precision and calm confidence — like a well-designed
instrument, not a dashboard full of flashing numbers.

## Anti-references

- Bloomberg Terminal: dense, chaotic, information overload as a status symbol.
- Crypto/Web3 neon aesthetic: high-contrast neons, dark-mode-by-default
  hype, glowing borders, gamified UI that signals speculation over craft.

## Design Principles

1. **Structure as clarity** — Every screen has an obvious hierarchy. The
   strategy canvas is the hero; surrounding chrome recedes.
2. **Expert approachability** — Professional enough for experienced traders;
   unintimidating for someone new to systematic strategies.
3. **Earned distinctiveness** — Stand apart through considered layout, type,
   and color choices — not decoration or novelty.
4. **Show results, hide complexity** — Surface real data (equity curves,
   trade counts, metrics) progressively. Never dump information.
5. **Transparent by design** — The tool is honest about its assumptions
   (fees, slippage, execution model). UI copy reflects this directness.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.2 AA compliance. Reduced-motion support via system preference.
Color is never the sole carrier of meaning (state indicators include
labels or icons alongside color).
